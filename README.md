
# E-Commerce Revenue and Profitability Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing e-commerce sales data to provide insights into revenue, profitability, customer purchasing behavior, and product performance. An interactive Power BI dashboard was developed to support business decision-making through data-driven insights.

---

## Business Problem

The business lacks a centralized reporting system to monitor revenue, profitability, product performance, and customer behavior. Existing reporting processes are manual, resulting in delayed insights and limited visibility into overall business performance.

---

## Project Objectives

* Analyze revenue and profit trends over time.
* Identify top-performing products and categories.
* Monitor customer purchasing behavior.
* Analyze state-wise sales performance.
* Evaluate payment method preferences.
* Track product return patterns.
* Enable data-driven business decision-making.

## Data Cleaning and Transformation

The following data preprocessing steps were performed:

* Handled missing values.
* Replaced missing values in the State column using mode imputation.
* Verified and removed duplicate records.
* Corrected data inconsistencies.
* Validated negative values in Quantity and Profit columns.
* Standardized data types.
* Created calculated columns for Year and Month analysis.
* Created DAX measures for KPI calculations.

## Business Questions

1. Which categories contribute the highest revenue and profit?
2. How has revenue changed over time?
3. Which states generate the highest revenue?
4. Which products are the top performers by revenue and profit?
5. Which payment methods are most preferred by customers?
6. What impact do product returns have on business performance?

---

## Key Business Insights

* Electronics category generated the highest revenue and profit.
* Tamil Nadu emerged as the highest revenue-generating state.
* Smart Watch was identified as the best-selling product.
* Debit Card was the most preferred payment method.
* The business achieved a healthy profit margin.
* Product returns indicate opportunities for improving customer satisfaction and product quality.

---

## Recommendations

* Increase investment in high-performing product categories.
* Focus marketing campaigns on top-performing states.
* Analyze reasons for product returns and implement corrective actions.
* Encourage customers to use preferred digital payment methods.
* Improve inventory planning during peak sales periods.
